The Butterfly Society of Virginia

Butterfly Society of Virginia (BSV) is a local non-profit organization whose mission is to raise the general consciousness of the public regarding the survival problems of butterflies and moths - through publicity, programs, and activities. WIth the western population of butterflies having declined by 90% their mission has become all the more urgent. Our research allowed us to learn that BSV needed to establish credibility through an improved online experience and diversify fundraising opportunities to ensure sustainability.

OURPLACE

OURPLACE is a safe and intuitive mobile application designed to help users find their ideal roommate(s) based on their specific needs and preferences. The surge in rent prices are causing young adults to seek roommates but the task of finding compatible, reliable, and trustworthy roommates has increasingly become stressful. OURPLACE offers advanced security screening, minimal UI and enhanced filters that range from preferred language spoken to lifestyle choices.

Case Study: Instructables

Since its founding in 2005, Instructables has grown into an amazing community of curious doers from around the world and is home to hundreds of thousands of projects. Through the UX research process our team found that users needed an on-line mobile-first platform that's intuitive, offers an improved searching experience, and organizes the user produced content without cluttering the homepage with endless choices.
