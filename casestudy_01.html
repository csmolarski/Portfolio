Overview:

The Butterfly Society of Virginia (BSV) is a local non-profit organization whose mission is to raise the general consciousness of the public regarding the survival problems of butterflies and moths - through publicity, programs, and activities. With an increased sense of environmental urgency, our team was tasked to rethink BSV’s online platform with the goal of improving their impact.

    • Challenge:

    We were challenged to help to help BSV establish credibility, increase engagement and diversify fundraising opportunities to ensure the organization's sustainability.

    Our Approach

    To define a strategy that increases BSV's impact
