Overview

The Butterfly Society of Virginia (BSV) is a local non-profit organization whose mission is to raise the general consciousness of the public regarding the survival problems of butterflies and moths - through publicity, programs, and activities. With an increased sense of environmental urgency, our team was tasked to rethink BSV’s online platform with the goal of improving their impact.

Process

  • research
  • define
  • ideation
  • prototype

    • Challenge

    We were challenged to help BSV establish credibility, increase engagement and diversify fundraising opportunities to ensure the organization's sustainability.

    Our Approach

    To define a strategy that increases BSV's impact

    Our team implemented various research and analysis methodologies to better understand how to establish trust with users, drive engagement by expanding the community's reach, and increase partnership oppurtunities.

    Research and ideation

  • Competitor Analysis
  • Heuristic Anaylis
  • Preliminary Data and Statistics
  • User Interviews and Insights
  • Persona
  • Journey Map
  • Mind Map
  • Mood Boards

    • “As a local business owner, I get involved with organizations … that offer a combination of credibility and perks” - Jessica B

    Driven by data and analysis, we were able to outline four essential areas of concentration:

  • Improve consistency & standards
  • Implement minimalist UI design
  • Emphasize mission statement and impact
  • Increase direct calls to action

    • Outcome

    Visual Design for Desktops and Mobile

    By understanding the evolving community's needs, and using a combination of best practices and creative-problem solving, we were able to redesign BSV’s platform to include a bold design, a clear environmental mission, increased calls to action and diversified partnership opportunities.

    Design

  • Low, mid and high fidelity wireframes
  • UI component development
  • User testing and validation
  • Style guides
